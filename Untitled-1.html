About Kaito Kid

Kaito Kid, also known as the Phantom Thief, is a master of disguise and illusion. He is a character from the Detective Conan series, known for his bold heists and brilliant mind.

History

Kaito Kid first appeared in the manga series "Magic Kaito," created by Gosho Aoyama. Over time, he has become one of the most iconic characters in the Detective Conan universe.
